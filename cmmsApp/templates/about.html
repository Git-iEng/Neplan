The NEPLAN Advantage

Modular, enterprise-grade analysis & planning for modern utilities

NEPLAN is a modular, enterprise-grade platform used to analyse, plan, optimise, and simulate power and other utility networks-available on cloud, intranet, or desktop to fit your IT strategy.

  1. 1

    Modular by design

    Pick just the calculation modules you need (e.g., Load Flow, Short-Circuit, Protection, Reliability, Harmonics, Dynamics) and add more as your use cases grow. Licensing is flexible-modules can be purchased individually.

  2. 2

    Cloud / Intranet / Desktop flexibility

    Run NEPLAN 360 in the cloud (anywhere via the NEPLAN app) or keep NEPLAN 10 on your intranet/desktop-same analysis pedigree, different deployment models.

  3. 3

    From basic design to asset management

    Start with core network studies (MV/LV/HV), then extend to operations and lifecycle decisions: online MV/LV monitoring with NEPLAN Anywhere, plus Asset Management and Strategic/Operational AM modules for risk-based planning and maintenance.

  4. 4

    Multi-utility, one platform

    Model electricity-and, when needed, simulate gas, water, and heating/cooling networks (even simultaneously) to study cross-sector interactions.

  5. 5

    Open interfaces for your stack

    Use NEPLAN as a web service (SaaS/SAS) to exchange data with GIS/SCADA/MDM and other enterprise apps; the NEPLAN server can run in the cloud or intranet.

  6. 6

    Standards & libraries built-in

    Work to international standards (IEC/ANSI/IEEE) with rich element and protection libraries, so studies are consistent and defensible.

  7. 7

    Made for office and field

    A simplified, browser-based UI in NEPLAN Anywhere lets teams capture data and run essentials on laptops, tablets, or phones-ideal for substation or site work.
