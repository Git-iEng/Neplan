Overview

NEPLAN Research - Education & Advanced Simulation Platform

NEPLAN Research provides a powerful environment for both education and research in power systems. Universities, engineering schools, and research institutions benefit from its intuitive graphical interface, comprehensive library of network elements, and ready-to-use examples based on internationally recognized standards and textbooks.

With capabilities ranging from load flow and short circuit analysis to real-time simulation, scripting, and cloud-based access, NEPLAN Research supports academic learning and advanced scientific studies. It offers free student versions, research licenses with up to 5000 nodes, and integrated scripting options for C# and Python, enabling customized automation and complex system modeling.

  • Academic-Friendly Interface

    Intuitive graphical user interface with pre-defined examples from IEC, IEEE, and leading power system textbooks, perfect for teaching and learning environments.

  • Advanced Research Tools

    Supports NEPLAN Simulation Language (NSL-SymDef), event definitions, and scripting in C# and Python for custom automation and analysis.

  • Cloud & On-Prem Options

    Free student version and research subscription licenses with up to 5000 nodes, accessible without any local installation.

  • Multi-User University Packages

    Includes licenses for teaching and research in a shared environment, with modules covering protection, reliability, stability, and optimization studies.

  • Integration & Scripting

    Interfaces with MatPower, supports real-time data analysis, and offers web services for external application integration and automation workflows.
