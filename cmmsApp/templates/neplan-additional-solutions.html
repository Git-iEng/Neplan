Overview

XGSLab – Grounding, Lightning & EMI Analysis

XGSLab is a state-of-the-art software platform designed for grounding, lightning, and electromagnetic interference (EMI) analysis in power and industrial systems. It is fully integrated into the NEPLAN ecosystem, providing engineers with advanced modeling capabilities to ensure electrical safety, reliability, and regulatory compliance.

  • Grounding System Design & Analysis

    Model and optimize earthing systems, from simple grounding electrodes to complex substation or plant layouts.

  • Lightning Protection Studies

    Evaluate risks from direct and indirect lightning strikes and optimize surge protection measures.

  • Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) Analysis

    Assess coupling between power systems and nearby telecommunication, signaling, or control systems to prevent operational disruptions.

  • Standards Compliance

    Built-in support for IEEE, IEC, EN and other international standards to ensure regulatory compliance.

  • Integration with NEPLAN Models

    Import electrical network data directly from NEPLAN for consistent design and analysis workflows.

Use Cases

Where XGSLab Delivers Value

Typical applications of XGSLab in grounding, lightning, and EMI analysis across power and industrial systems.

  • Substation & Power Plant Grounding

    Design and optimize grounding systems for substations and power plants to ensure safety and compliance.

  • Lightning Protection Studies

    Implement protection strategies for industrial facilities against direct and indirect lightning strikes.

  • EMI Mitigation Solutions

    Analyze and reduce electromagnetic interference in railways, pipelines, or telecommunication networks.

  • HV/MV/LV Safety Assessments

    Conduct safety studies for high, medium, and low voltage networks to ensure operational reliability.
